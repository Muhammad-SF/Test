Odoo 10 Target Based Sales Commission

Easily Manage Complex Commission or Incentive Plans to based on Goals and Targets assign to a team or a sales man.

Globalteckz Sales Commission/Incentive Management module based on Targets allows businesses to design, track and pay sales commission plans, based on the targets or goals you set for each sales person. You can set the commission based on amount as well as on percentage basis. This Odoo app helps you and your sales team to be in line with the targets they are achieving and the commission they are
We have already developed Sales Commission module based on sales done, Payment received, and invoicing done. But there are certain countries or organization where target based commission method is use where a Salesman is provided with a monthly target or range of target further achieving those range the salesman is entitled for the commission set up by the company.
Our Sales incentive module helps sales teams get a clear view into their incentives through automated commission reporting, ensuring that sales reps understand their incentives and are aligned with business goals.
